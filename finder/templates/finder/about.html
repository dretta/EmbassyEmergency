About

EmbassyEmergency is an minimalist app that allows you to find Embassies all over the world. You can either search for a specific Embassy based on it's government of origin and destination country, or browse through the countries and see what dipolmatic missions they have sent out.

EmbassyEmergency is built using Django, Bootstrap, and PostgresSQL. Click here to view the source code. Daniel Retta is the sole Developer of EmbassyEmergency, visit the Contact page if you wish to reach him.
